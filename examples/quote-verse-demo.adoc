= Quote & Verse Blocks
:toc:

== Quote Blocks

A quote block wraps content in a `blockquote` and optionally attributes it to an author and source.

=== Simple Quote

____
The only way to do great work is to love what you do.
____

=== Attributed Quote

[quote, Abraham Lincoln, Gettysburg Address]
____
Four score and seven years ago our fathers brought forth on this continent,
a new nation, conceived in Liberty, and dedicated to the proposition
that all men are created equal.
____

=== Quote with Nested Content

[quote, Donald Knuth, The Art of Computer Programming]
____
Premature optimization is the root of all evil.

The real problem is that programmers have spent far too much time worrying
about efficiency in the wrong places and at the wrong times.
____

== Verse Blocks

A verse block preserves line breaks, making it suitable for poetry and song lyrics.

=== Poetry

[verse, Carl Sandburg, Fog]
____
The fog comes
on little cat feet.

It sits looking
over harbor and city
on silent haunches
and then moves on.
____

=== Song Lyrics

[verse, William Blake, The Tyger]
____
Tyger Tyger, burning bright,
In the forests of the night;
What immortal hand or eye,
Could frame thy fearful symmetry?
____

=== Verse without Attribution

[verse]
____
To be, or not to be,
that is the question.
____
